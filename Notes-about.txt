you should know that the configured cli commands are :

'addNote' (noteTitle, noteBody)    : to add a new note .
'saveNotes'                        : to save all notes .
'removeNote' (noteTitle)           : to remove a specific note .
'listNotes'                        : to list all saved notes .
'readNote' (noteTitle)             : to read a specific note .
